Academic December 10, 2024

Board Exam Preparation Drive for Class 10

KPIS launched a comprehensive board examination preparation programme for Class 10 students. The drive includes intensive coaching sessions, subject-wise revision classes, weekly mock tests based on RBSE patterns, and one-on-one doubt-clearing sessions with teachers. Special focus is being given to Maths, Science, and English. Parents have been kept informed through regular meetings and progress reports.

Environment October 5, 2024

World Environment Day ‚Äì Tree Plantation Drive

On the occasion of World Environment Day, KPIS students and teachers participated in a tree plantation drive on the school campus. Over 50 saplings were planted, with students adopting individual plants to care for. The event aimed to instil environmental consciousness in young minds. Students also created awareness posters and presented skits on the importance of protecting our environment.
