Our History

A Decade of Educational Excellence

KP International School was founded in 2015 with a singular vision — to bring world-class, English-medium education to the rural heartland of Rajasthan. Located in Morija Village, approximately 4 km from Chomu town and 35 km north of Jaipur, the school was established to fill a critical gap in quality education in this region.

From its humble beginnings with a handful of students and dedicated teachers, KPIS has grown into a thriving co-educational institution serving students from Nursery through Class 10. Affiliated with the Rajasthan Board of Secondary Education (RBSE), the school has built a strong reputation for academic rigour combined with character development.

Over the years, KPIS has invested heavily in modern infrastructure, smart classrooms, a well-stocked library, and sports facilities — ensuring that every student has the resources needed to excel academically and grow personally.
